Kubecost
¶
kubecost helps you monitor cost inside a single kubernetes cluster.
SRE team
does not support
the paid verion of kubecost that has multi-cluster cost visibility.
Cluster configuration
¶
In order to configure
kube-prometheus-stack
in your cluster, follow this
generic example
, for your
sre-baseapps-configs*
instance.
Note that the prometheus service name (from the
kube-prometheus-stack
baseapp)
must
be included. It can be found in ArgoCD, by filtering on
Service
resources.
The service itself will have the
app: kube-prometheus-stack-prometheus
label in its live manifest.
