SCF-IAM-01.01
csdl secrets management
Applications deployed on ET&I Platform can access secrets of other applications or secrets that used for managing the underlying infrastructure
¶
Description
¶
The ET&I Platform uses external-secrets for syncronizing Kubernetes Secret resources from she secret storage (Keeper).
It is possible to deploy such a configuration that can be used for accessing secrets of other applications or secrets that used for managing the underlying infrastructure. This can lead to a wide range of security problems and possible risks.
Business Impacts
¶
Customer Confidentiality
Service Compromise
Likelihood: Medium
¶
Risk Level Category: High
