SCF-BCR-01.01
SSL certificate
Disruption in Let's Encrypt certificate issuer's service may lead to downtime of ET&I Platform hosted websites and applications
¶
Description
¶
The ET&I Platform relies on Let's Encrypt and the Kubernetes cert-manager to obtain SSL certificates for HTTPS.
Disruption in Let's Encrypt's services or exceeding the services quotas may render unaccessible the websites and web applications to end users.

¶
