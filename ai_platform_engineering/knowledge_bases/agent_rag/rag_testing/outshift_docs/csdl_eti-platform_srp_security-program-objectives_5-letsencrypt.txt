BCR-01.01
SSL
Accept certificate issuer (Let's Encrypt) related risk
¶
Target Date: FY??Q?
¶
Risk(s) Addressed
¶
Disruption in Let's Encrypt certificate issuer's service may lead to downtime of ET&I Platform hosted websites and applications
Description
¶
The ET&I Platform relies on the third party service Let's Encypt as certificate issuer
for SSL certificates.
Although a few downtimes happened in the past, the chances that these can affect the business continuity of the ET&I Platform hosted applications is negligible.
Definition of Done
¶
The ET&I SRE team proposes to accept this risk at this maturity stage of the Platform.
No further action required at this point.
Relevant Controls
¶
BCR-01.01
Objective Status: Accept Risk
