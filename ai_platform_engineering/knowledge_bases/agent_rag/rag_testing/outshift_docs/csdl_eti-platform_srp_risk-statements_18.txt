IVS-13.03
publishing systems
Third party publishing systems publicly accessible on the Internet may have currently unspotted vulnerabilities that can be exploited and can lead to customer data exposure or unauthorized access
¶
Description
¶
Some of the publishing systems (e.g. Harbor) used by the ETI Platform are publicly accessible on the Internet.
These third party components may have currently unspotted vulnerabilities that can be exploited.
Currently there are no measures applied to prevent (penetration testing and patching) or mitigate (WAF solutions, network restrictions) the possible attacks on these components.
Business Impacts
¶
Service Compromise
Likelihood: Low
¶
Risk Level Category: Medium
