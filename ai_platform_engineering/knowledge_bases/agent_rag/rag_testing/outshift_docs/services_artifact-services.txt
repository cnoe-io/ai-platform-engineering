Artifact Services
¶
Artifacts are one of the outputs of a CI build process. Artifact management includes highly available storage, distribution, retrieval of the artifacts with appropriate RBAC controls for internal and external users.
RBAC controls for external users include
Anonymous access
Authenticated access

Intermediate shared libraries, modules, packages
