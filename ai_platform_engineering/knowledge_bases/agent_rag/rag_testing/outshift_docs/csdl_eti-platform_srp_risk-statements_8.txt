BCR-08.01
Inadequate database backup validation hinders disaster recovery
¶
Description
¶
The ETI SRE team currently does not have tools and processes for monitoring database backup quality of for testing those backups regularly.
Business Impacts
¶
Business Continuity
Customer Data Loss
Production database clusters run on AWS Aurora and span multiple availability zones. The databases themselves have extremely limited access. However, these measures do not preclude internal mishaps or recovery in case of regional failure.
Likelihood: Low
¶
Risk Level Category: Medium
