Monitoring solution used by ET&I Platform might be not suitable for ensuring business continuity requirements and uncertain future of development can be sources of potential security risks
¶
Description
¶
The ET&I Platform uses MCOM (formerly One Eye) as the primary solution for monitoring, alerting, metrics, and log pipelines.
MCOM currently not suitable for ensuring some business continuity requirements (e.g. multi-cluster monitoring).
Furthermore considering that MCOM is not a well-established product, if Cisco's interest to discontinue the product or take the development into a different direction that could be a source of additonal security risks.
