DSP-01.02
Production systems inventories are insufficiently detailed
¶
The ETI SRE team maintained inventory has gaps. For example, it does not contain data regarding:
S3 buckets, their purpose, and ownership
Database clusters
Databases, their purpose, and ownership
Business Impacts
¶
Business Continuity
Customer Data Loss
Likelihood: High
¶
Resources are known to be inadequately or not documented.
Risk Level Category: Medium
¶
The primary risk for inadequate inventories is slowing down recovery of a resource or resources. However, informal, tribal knowledge amongst the SRE team mitigates the potential for complete failure of that recovery.
