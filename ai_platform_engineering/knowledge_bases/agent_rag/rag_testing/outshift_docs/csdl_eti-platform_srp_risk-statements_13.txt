AWS telemetry is insufficiently integrated to central monitoring and alerting
¶
Description
¶
AWS resources are monitored through a variety, but that telemetry is not adequately incorporated into the entirety of the Platform's monitoring suite. Those gaps include:
Bastion logs
EC2 instance monitoring
RDS logs
RDS monitoring (replication lag, number of replicas)
SES quotas
SES complaints and bounces
EBS volume metrics (volume failures)
