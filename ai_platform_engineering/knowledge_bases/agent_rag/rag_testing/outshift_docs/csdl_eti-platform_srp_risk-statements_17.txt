Possible gaps not covered by Intrusion Detection Systems may inhibit the detection and effective reaction to malicious activity
¶
Description
¶
Currently it is unclear which areas covered by Cisco internal security teams in the different layers (cloud provider, network, host, etc) of the ETI SRE Platform's infrastructure in terms of intrusion or malicious activity detection.
Consequently further examination needed to identify the gaps and the areas that fall under the SRE team's responsibility.
