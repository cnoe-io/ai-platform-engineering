Description
¶
There are multiple third-party applications hosted under the .eticloud.io root domain, some of them with inadequate cookie security settings.
This provides attack surface for cross-subdomain cookie attacks, information leakage through cookies, and other type of attacks that depends on cookies.
In a case a third-party application was identified that leaked internal information via sending them to an external analytics service and had potential access to other cookies set by other internal systems.
