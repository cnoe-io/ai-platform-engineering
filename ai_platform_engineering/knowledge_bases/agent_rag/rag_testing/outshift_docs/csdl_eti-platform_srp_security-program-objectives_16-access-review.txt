IAM-01.01
Perform regular access reviews and revoke unnecessary privileges
¶
Target Date: FY??Q?
¶
Risk(s) Addressed
¶
Unnecessarily broad permissions may lead to privilege escalation
Description
¶
Perform regular access reviews (either for users and applications) and revoke unnecessary privileges considering the least privilege principle.
Limit the capability of applications to be executed as users with elevated privileges.
Provide recommendations and best practices for the venture teams about access and privilege management.
Definition of Done
¶
Regular access reviews are performed and unnecessary privileges revoked.

¶
