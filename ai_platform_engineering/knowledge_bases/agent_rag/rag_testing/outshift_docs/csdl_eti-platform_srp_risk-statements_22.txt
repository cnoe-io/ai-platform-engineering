SCF-BCR-01.01
secret storage
Secret storage service (Keeper) degradation may result in failures amongst applications that relies on secrets
¶
Description
¶
The ET&I Platform relies on the Cisco STO managed Keeper as a secret storage. Secrets are syncronized in certain time periods to Kubernetes secret objects by the external-secrets Kubernetes operator. In an event of Keeper service degradation if external-secrets tries to syncronize secrets, that can lead to degraded Secret objects and application failures.
