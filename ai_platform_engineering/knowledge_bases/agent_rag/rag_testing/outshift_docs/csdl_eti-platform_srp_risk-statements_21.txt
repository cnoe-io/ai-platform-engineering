SCF-CEK-04.01
secret storage
Current implementation of secret storage exposes secrets those who has administrative access to Kubernetes clusters
¶
Description
¶
The ET&I Platform utilizes the external-secrets Kubernetes operator to make secrets stored in Vault available for application deployed on Kubernetes clusters.
The current implementation gives access to these secrets those to have adminsitrative access to the Kubernetes clusters, regardless whether they have privileges to these secrets in Vault or not.

¶
Customer Confidentiality
Likelihood: Low
