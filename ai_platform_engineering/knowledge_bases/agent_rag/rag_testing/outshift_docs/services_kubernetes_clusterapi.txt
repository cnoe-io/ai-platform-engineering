P3 ClusterAPI K8s
¶
P3 is Cisco IT hosted Openstack in internal datacenter
ClusterAPI is a Kubernetes sub-project focused on providing declarative APIs and tooling to simplify provisioning, upgrading, and operating multiple Kubernetes clusters.
ClusterAPI support Openstack as one of the providers
