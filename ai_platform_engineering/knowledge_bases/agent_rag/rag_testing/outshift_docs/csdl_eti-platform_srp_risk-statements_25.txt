SCF-IAM-02.02
monitoring
Production monitoring system is publicly accessible from the Internet without Cisco VPN connection and may be vulnerable to man-in-the-middle attacks or zero-day vulnerabilities in the third-party authentication system
¶
Description
¶
Some components of the ET&I Platform's monitoring system (MCOM) are accessible from the Internet without Cisco VPN connection. Even if these components are restricted by an authentication mechanism, they might be still vulnerable to man-in-the-middle attacks or zero-day vulnerabilities in the third-party authentication system.
Business Impacts
¶
Service Compromise
Likelihood: Low
¶
Risk Level Category: Medium
