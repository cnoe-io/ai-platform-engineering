Possible gaps in employee offboarding process may lead to unauthorized access to restricted systems
¶
Description
¶
The ET&I SRE team currently does not have a well-established offboarding processes for SRE team members and users for the ET&I Platform.
Gaps in these processes may lead to unauthorized access to restricted systems.
