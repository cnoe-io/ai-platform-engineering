To wire the slack-bot subchart to the same client_secret as Keycloak (the
recommended single-source-of-truth setup), add this to your umbrella values:

  slack-bot:
    oauth2:
      clientSecretFromSecret:
        name: {{ include "keycloak.botSecretName" . }}
        key: KC_BOT_CLIENT_SECRET

To wire the webex-bot subchart to the same Webex bot client_secret as Keycloak:

  webex-bot:
    keycloakBot:
      clientSecretFromSecret:
        name: {{ include "keycloak.webexBotSecretName" . }}
        key: KC_WEBEX_BOT_CLIENT_SECRET
